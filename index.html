Our Mission

At Newcastle University Women in Engineering (NUWIE), our mission is to support and highlight the women in engineering. We believe in building a stronger industry through diversity, and we welcome anyone—regardless of gender—who supports our mission to join us in building a more inclusive future for the profession. We provide a platform for networking, professional development, and community building, ensuring every student has the tools and confidence to excel in the engineering world.

From industry workshops to social mixers, our society is a vibrant hub for collaboration and inspiration. Join us as we break barriers and build a more inclusive future for engineering.
